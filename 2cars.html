2 Cars



2 Cars is a 2D endless mobile adventurous game. This game is designed and developed using Unity game engine. The objective is to control both red and blue car at the simultaneously and collect points ie (blue and red circles) on the way as much as possible while avoiding various road-blocks. Its a demo game created from the original 2 Cars on play store.
